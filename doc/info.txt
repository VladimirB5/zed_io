Modul control ZEDboard LEDS, buttons and switches,
when button or switches is pressed, interrupt can be generated.
To every button and switch debouncer also can be connected (enabled by default).

Simple character driver for linux and example of user space app is avaliable.

Version of kernel and gcc:
kernel : linux-xilinx-v2022.1 (tag)
gcc    : arm-buildroot-linux-uclibcgnueabihf-gcc.br_real (Buildroot 2022.02.1) 10.3.0
